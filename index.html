Digital Behavior: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts

Journey Stage: Mid-to-late (actively searching: "I need answers now")

üïäÔ∏è The Grieving Soul

Digital Behavior: YouTube (guided content), Facebook groups, Pinterest, Google

Searches: "Signs from the afterlife," "How to talk to someone who passed"

Journey Stage: Late stage (ready to speak with a medium)

üß≠ The Life Path Seeker

Digital Behavior: YouTube, Reddit, Instagram Carousels, Medium, Podcasts

Searches: "Should I quit my job?" "What is my soul purpose?"

Journey Stage: Early to mid (researching spiritual resources)

ü§î The Curious Skeptic

Digital Behavior: Reddit, Google (FAQ-focused), YouTube explainers

Searches: Stumbles into psychic content via influencers or testimonials

Journey Stage: Early (needs education, credibility before converting)
