👥 Target Personas

💔 The Heartbroken Seeker

Demographics: Age 25-40, Female, Income $35K-$65K, Single/newly separated

Pain Points: Recent breakup, ghosting, toxic relationship cycles. Wants closure, reassurance, or to know "if he's coming back"

Digital Behavior: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts

Searches: "Why did he leave me?" "Twin flame runner meaning"

Journey Stage: Mid-to-late (actively searching: "I need answers now")

🕊️ The Grieving Soul

Demographics: Age 35-55, Female, Income $50K-$90K, Suburban, caretaker role

Pain Points: Loss of parent, sibling, child, partner, or pet. Experiences "weird dreams" or sense someone is still near

Digital Behavior: YouTube (guided content), Facebook groups, Pinterest, Google

Searches: "Signs from the afterlife," "How to talk to someone who passed"

Journey Stage: Late stage (ready to speak with a medium)

🧭 The Life Path Seeker

Demographics: Age 30-50, Mixed gender, Income $45K-$90K, College educated, career-stalled

Pain Points: Feels lost, burned out, "meant for more." Job transitions, quarter/midlife crisis

Digital Behavior: YouTube, Reddit, Instagram Carousels, Medium, Podcasts

Searches: "Should I quit my job?" "What is my soul purpose?"

Journey Stage: Early to mid (researching spiritual resources)

🤔 The Curious Skeptic

Demographics: Age 25-40, Mixed gender, Income $40K-$85K, Tech-savvy, analytical

Pain Points: High-pressure life moments when logic fails. Looking for new tools during crisis

Digital Behavior: Reddit, Google (FAQ-focused), YouTube explainers

Searches: Stumbles into psychic content via influencers or testimonials

Journey Stage: Early (needs education, credibility before converting)

🎯 Crisis Response Best Practices

❌ Never Do

• Exploit tragedy for marketing purposes

• Mention specific suicide methods

• Make false promises or guarantees

• Take political sides on controversial events

• Reproduce copyrighted content

✅ Always Do

• Lead with genuine support and empathy

• Include crisis resources when appropriate

• Validate emotions before offering solutions

• Provide hope without toxic positivity

• Respect cultural and religious sensitivities

⚠️ Sensitivity Guidelines

• Death/suicide: Maximum compassion, include resources

• Economic: Focus on spiritual vs material security

• Relationships: Address fears without feeding paranoia

• Natural disasters: Honor those affected

• Mental health: Combine professional help with spiritual guidance

🎯 Conversion Guidelines

• Natural transition to psychic guidance services

• Match CTA intensity to crisis sensitivity level

• Use crisis-appropriate timing for offers

• Avoid appearing exploitative of vulnerable moments

• Focus on genuine help first, conversion second
